Algebra CP Problem
May 19, 2024

Link: https://codeforces.com/contest/1618/problem/E Essentially the problem asks to solve a system of equations: $$ \begin{align*} 1a_1 + 2a_2 + 3a_3 + \ldots + na_n &= b_1\tag{1}\\ 2a_1 + 3a_2 + 4a_3 + \ldots + 1a_n &= b_2\tag{2}\\ &\space\space \vdots\\ na_1 + 1a_2 + 2a_3 + \ldots + (n-1)a_n &= b_n\tag{n} \end{align*} $$ Traditional methods to solve systems are too slow for $n\leq 2\cdot 10^5$. But the structure of the equations means such general algorithms are not needed.
